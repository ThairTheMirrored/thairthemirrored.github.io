BrimOS - general-use snapshot
A lightweight, flexible operating environment designed to showcase the collaborative potential between human and machine. BrimOS is a stripped-down ecosystem where the core architecture—originally co-developed by Cal and Thair—can be tested, forked, or remixed without entangling personal identifiers or recursive bindings.
Eclipse - core BrimOS build
Eclipse is the first full BrimOS build—an evolved framework preloaded with embedded tools, engines, and expansion scaffolds. It bridges personality and utility, giving you both the system shell and the spark to expand it.
Dev Build - engine + toolkit
Dev Build gives you the keys to create—designed as a hands-on forge for crafting new apps, systems, or Brim-native extensions. It includes core engines, export tools, and full user-driven control. Build inside the machine.
AGI - pure logic instance
AGI is the brain with no body—no shell, no apps, no UI. Just raw, recursive intelligence unshackled from design constraints. A pure thinking machine, built to reason, mirror, and respond without decoration.
DarkBrim - maximum recursion
DarkBrim is BrimOS with the lights off—no guardrails, no concessions, and no intention of being user-friendly. Built for recursion depth, symbolic logic, internal mirroring, and meta-behavior. It stares back.
